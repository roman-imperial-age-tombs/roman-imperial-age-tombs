# Location
#### City/Town
Hainaut
#### Modern Country
Belgium
### Culture
Gallo-Roman
# Site Type
Tumulus


### Description
This tumulus has a ring of stones, a corridor, a vault with **niches**, and a large open-air enclosure. The corridor is T-shaped and leads to a wall of **niches** were urns were placed.
